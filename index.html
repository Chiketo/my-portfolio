06 / Publications

Research

Peer-reviewed papers, reports, and written contributions.

  • 2024 · Journal of Health Informatics in Developing Countries
    Federated Health Information Systems in Low-Resource Settings: Architecture, Trade-offs, and Implementation Lessons
    Vol. 18, No. 2, pp. 45–62 · Whatmore, C., & [Co-author]
    This paper examines the architectural and governance trade-offs of federated versus centralised health data systems in sub-Saharan Africa, drawing on a two-year implementation study across three countries.
  • 2023 · African Journal of Information Systems
    Data Quality Dimensions in National Health Management Information Systems: Evidence from Zimbabwe
    Vol. 15, No. 3 · Whatmore, C.
    An empirical evaluation of data quality across 8 dimensions in Zimbabwe's HMIS, providing recommendations for data governance structures to improve accuracy and completeness.
  • 2023 · Conference Proceedings — IEEE Informatics Africa
    Geospatial Deprivation Indexing for Urban Policy: A Case Study of Harare
    pp. 112–119 · Whatmore, C., et al.
    Proposes a composite geospatial deprivation index methodology using open-source data and applies it to Harare metropolitan area, demonstrating its utility for equitable resource allocation.
  • 2022 · International Journal of Medical Informatics
    Mobile Health Data Collection in Rural Settings: Reliability, Completeness, and Design Implications
    Vol. 167 · Whatmore, C. & [Co-authors]
    Analysis of 50,000 mobile-collected health records to assess how network conditions, device literacy, and form design affect data completeness in rural community health worker programmes.
  • 2022 · Policy Brief
    Towards a National Data Strategy for Zimbabwe: Recommendations for Health and Social Sectors
    Submitted to Ministry of ICT and Ministry of Health · Whatmore, C. & Team
    A policy brief offering actionable recommendations for a coherent national data governance strategy, addressing interoperability, data sovereignty, and capacity building.
